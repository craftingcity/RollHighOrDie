# Preamble
As a Player in the game of RHoD, you will act as a Character that you will work with the Referee to create. The Referee will be acting as anything and everything else, including the final interpretation of the rules at their table.

### Step By Step
1. Roll & Assign Ability Scores
	-. Roll 4d6 and d, assign the array to your 6 Ability Scores.
2. Introduce Character Concept
3. Spend 4 [[Experience]]
	- It is recommended that you attain at least one [[Features]], one [[Traits]], and one or more Combat [[Skills]] for creating your first Character.
4. Acquire Equipment
