# Preamble
Emboldenments are the positive effects imparted by one creature onto another. 

## Mundane Emboldenments
#### Aided
While you are 'Aided', when you make an Ability Check, the creature that granted you this Emboldenment makes the same Ability Check. You may take either result. Then, you Naturally Resolve this Emboldentment.

If you are ever more than 5' away from the creature that granted you this Emboldenment, you Manually Resolve this Emboldenment
