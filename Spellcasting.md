# Preamble
The ability to manipulate reality on a personal whim is known as "Magic", and the induvial manipulations are known as "Spells". The people or entities that can perform this "Magic" are known as "Spellcasters".

## Understanding Spells
Spells simply are the combination of a desired effect and magical effort. Some Spellcasters gather pre-defined Spells 

#### Techniques
In the context of Magic, Techniques are groups of defined Spells or Spell Effects. Often, a Spellcaster will have access to multiple Techniques while specializing in a select few.
