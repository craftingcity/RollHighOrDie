# Preamble
The following Conditions, Ailments, and Emboldenments are related to explicitly magical occurrences or effects.

### Conditions
#### Flux Overpressured
While you are 'Flux Overpressured', at the beginning of your turn you take a number of Radiant Damage equal to your current total Flux minus your Flux Capacity for each stack of 'Flux Overpressured' you have.

On your turn, if your current total Flux is less than your Flux Capacity, you may spend 2 AP to Manually Resolve a stack of 'Flux Overpressured'.

### Ailments
#### Irreparable
While you are 'Irreparable', you cannot regain Vitality.

### Emboldenments
#### Guided
While you are 'Guided', when you next make a Skill Check, you may add 1d4 to the result. Then, you Naturally Resolve a stack of 'Guided'.
