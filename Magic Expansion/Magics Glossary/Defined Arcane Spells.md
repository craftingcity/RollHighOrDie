## CATEGOREY
### Classical Wizardry
#### Magic Missile
Complexity: 12 (4+4+0+4)
Effort: 4 (1+2+0+1)
Speed: 2

**Projectile, Long Range, Sphere (1'), Immediate, Damage (Force)**.
Target a creature or point within 90' of you and establish Line of Sight.

Make a Casting Skill Check against your Target's Reflex.

If you hit, you deal 1d8 force damage.

#### Booming Hold
Complexity: 16 (4+0+0+6+2+4)
Effort: 8 (1+0+1+4+1+1)
Speed: 4

**Projectile, Touch Range, Sphere (1'), Until Triggered, Minute Long, Damage (Force)**
Target a creature within 5' of you and establish Line of Sight.

Make a Casting Skill Check against your Target's Reflex.

If you hit, for the next minute, when that creature makes a Maneuver, they take 1d8 force damage.
